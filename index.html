3. Location Data

TideScout requests access to your device's precise location (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION) for one purpose only: to identify your nearest watersport spot on first launch. After that, your chosen location is saved locally on your device. We do not continuously track your location, we do not share your location with third parties, and we do not transmit your coordinates to our servers.

You can deny location permission and manually select any of our 109+ locations instead. You can reset your saved location at any time via Settings → Reset saved location within the app.

5. Data Storage and Security

All your personal data — saved spots, gear log, and preferences — is stored exclusively on your device using Android's standard secure storage. This data is not transmitted to our servers and is not accessible to us. It is removed when you uninstall the app or use Settings → Reset all preferences.

Weather forecasts are cached temporarily on your device to allow offline viewing and reduce data usage. This cache contains only weather data, not personal information.

6. Children's Privacy

TideScout is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

7. Your Rights (UK GDPR)

As a UK resident you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of data we hold about you
  • Erasure — request deletion of your data. For locally stored data, use Settings → Reset all preferences. For any data held by third-party services, refer to their respective policies above.
  • Restriction — object to certain processing of your data
  • Portability — receive your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent — disable location permission via Android Settings → Apps → TideScout → Permissions at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address in §1. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).

8. Data Retention

Data stored on your device persists until you uninstall the app or reset preferences manually. Anonymous analytics events (if enabled) are retained by PostHog for up to 12 months. We do not retain any other data.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy. Significant changes will be communicated via an in-app notice.
